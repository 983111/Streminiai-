# Privacy Policy for Stremini AI

## Last Updated: February 2026

### Overview

Stremini AI ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

### 1. Information We Collect

#### Personal Information

- **User Input**: Text messages and attachments you send through the chatbot feature
- **Device Information**: Device model, OS version, and app version
- **Usage Data**: Features you interact with, frequency of use, and duration

#### Sensitive Permissions

- **Accessibility Service**: Used only to scan for scam content on your screen for protection
- **Overlay Permission**: Used to display floating assistance bubbles over other apps
- **Camera**: Used only when you explicitly select to attach images
- **File Access**: Used only when you explicitly select files to attach
- **Notifications**: For security alerts and important updates

### 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide scam detection and screen analysis services
- Train and improve our AI models
- Send security alerts when threats are detected
- Maintain app functionality and performance
- Comply with legal obligations
- Prevent fraud and abuse

### 3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure local storage of sensitive data
- No storage of personal data without your consent
- Regular security updates and vulnerability patches
- ProGuard code obfuscation to prevent reverse engineering

### 4. Data Sharing

We do **NOT** share your personal information with third parties except:

- Our AI backend service (for analysis purposes)
- Legal authorities (when required by law)
- Service providers who help operate our app

### 5. User Rights & Control

You have the right to:

- Access your data
- Request deletion of your data
- Disable accessibility features at any time
- Revoke permissions in Android settings
- Opt-out of analytics

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@stremini.ai

### 6. Data Retention

- Chat data: Stored during active session, can be manually cleared
- Analytics: Retained for 90 days then automatically deleted
- Accessibility logs: Not stored, processed in real-time only
- Attachments: Deleted after processing and not stored long-term

### 7. Children's Privacy

Stremini AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it immediately.

### 8. Third-Party Services

This app uses:

- **Google Play Services**: For app updates and compatibility
- **OkHttp**: For secure network communication
- **Flutter Framework**: For app functionality

These services have their own privacy policies.

### 9. Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for:

- Maintaining confidentiality of your data
- Not sharing sensitive information with the chatbot
- Reviewing permission requests before granting
- Keeping your device secure

### 10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting. Your continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

### 11. Contact Us

For privacy concerns or requests:

- Email: privacy@stremini.ai
- In-app: Help > Contact Us
